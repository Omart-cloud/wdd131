Omart Clean - Site Plan

Site Name

Name: Omart Clean

Description: Omart Clean is a professional cleaning service that specializes in residential and commercial cleaning solutions. We provide deep cleaning, office sanitization, and eco-friendly cleaning services to keep your environment spotless.

Site Purpose

The Omart Clean website serves as an online hub where customers can learn about our cleaning services, book appointments, view pricing, and read testimonials from satisfied clients.

Scenarios

  • How can I book a professional cleaning service online?
  • What are the benefits of eco-friendly cleaning products?

Color Schema

Primary Color: Deep Green (#005A32) - Used for headings and navigation to represent freshness and cleanliness.

Accent Color: Soft Blue (#A7C7E7) - Used for buttons and call-to-action sections.

Typography

Heading Font: Poppins (used for all headings)

Body Font: Lato (used for paragraphs and general text)
